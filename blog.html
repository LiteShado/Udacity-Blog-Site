6 a.m. Thoughts

Morning Motivation

Let's talk about motivation, and how we are supposed to keep ourselves pushed to the limits EVEN during the most challenging moments of our lives. I find that it is always fulfilling to figure out what is slowing me down by meditating and journaling my thoughts. There's a way to tap into one's innermost desires by allowing yourself to listen to your own ideas. Sometimes, you need to unplug from the world around you and not get carried away in the rush of your environment. I truly feel that the physical feeling of stress is our body's way of speaking to us and letting us know we need to get in touch with our spirits. I love to meditate, it's where I feel the most necessary epiphanies lie.
